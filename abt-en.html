Tradition and Knowledge

Decades of Educational Tradition

The roots of our school date back to 1945, a period of renewal and laying the foundations of modern secondary education in Negotino. Through the decades, this institution has grown into a pillar of knowledge, where thousands of young minds began their professional paths. Officially as the "St. Cyril and Methodius" Gymnasium, the school began its mission in 1961, nurturing the enlightenment values of our patrons.

In its rich chronology, the school center proudly bore the name C.U.S.O. "Josip Broz Tito", a period in which high standards were set in technical and general education. Today, we are evolving into a multidisciplinary center that unites high school, electrical engineering, chemical, and economic education under one roof, adapting to the needs of the modern world.
